Product Details


The Supreme x Nike SB Dunk Low “Stars - Hyper Blue” is one of four March 2021 colorways inspired by the “World Famous” streetwear brand’s collaboration with Nike SB from 2003. In 2002, Supreme and Nike SB joined forces on an SB Dunk Low inspired by the Air Jordan 3 that borrowed the model’s elephant print branding and placed it on the overlaid panels of the then-new SB Dunk. A year later, in 2003, Supreme and Nike SB renewed its partnership with an SB Dunk High “Stars” in three colorways, which supplies the design language of the low-top edition of the “Stars” design. For this "Hyper Blue" colorway, white leather can be found on the perforated toe and on the mid-panel, collar, and heel. Overlaid Hyper Blue faux crocodile-textured panels on the forefoot, eyelets, collar, heel, and Swoosh provide a burst of color against the neutral base. Elsewhere, Supreme revives its “Stars” design on the mid-panel and dresses the Nike embroidery on the back of the shoe in the same metallic gold hue. A metallic gold Supreme lace emblem sits on the black laces of the white mesh tongue. Release date: March 4, 2021.
